About Us

SoundShockAudio

SoundShockAudio, founded by accomplished music producer and Pyramind Music Production School graduate Daniel Strongin, stands as a beacon for music producers seeking top-quality production tools without the hefty price tag. With a rich background as a mixing engineer, mastering engineer, and a contributor to renowned music production sites like The Pro Audio Files, Envato, and Producer Spot, Daniel brings a wealth of expertise and insight to SoundShockAudio. The platform is a treasure trove of over 4,000 free music production resources, including DAW templates, VST plugins, synth presets, sample packs, Kontakt Instruments, and MIDI, all meticulously curated to meet the highest standards of sound quality. Aimed at empowering music creators at every level, SoundShockAudio eliminates the tedious search for production tools, offering a one-stop archive that continuously evolves with the latest in music production technology. Perfect for those looking to enhance their sound, explore new techniques, or optimize their workflow, SoundShockAudio is more than just a resource; it's a community where passion for music and creativity thrives.
